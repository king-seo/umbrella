Cast-Main

  • Ellen Page as Vanya Hargreeves / Number Seven / The White Violin. Constantly told she had no supernatural abilities and was nothing special. In reality, she is actually the most powerful and dangerous of her siblings, so much that their father purposely suppressed and hid her powers from even herself.
  • Tom Hopper as Luther Hargreeves / Number One / Spaceboy, an astronaut with super strength. He lived on the moon for four years as a mission from his father. He was the only one of his siblings who did not leave the team and during a mission was severely injured. To save his life, Reginald injected him with a serum that turned his upper body into that of an ape's.[4]
  • David Castañeda as Diego Hargreeves / Number Two / The Kraken, a rebellious troublemaker with enhanced breath-holding abilities and gifted knife-throwing techniques.[4]
  • Emmy Raver-Lampman as Allison Hargreeves / Number Three / The Rumor, a celebrity with the ability to manipulate reality by lying with the phrase "I heard a rumor...".[4][5]
  • Robert Sheehan as Klaus Hargreeves / Number Four / The Séance, a drug addict with the ability to communicate with the dead.[4]
  • Aidan Gallagher as The Boy / Number Five, a boy with the ability to jump through space and time. After traveling to the future, he became stuck in a post-apocalyptic world, unable to get back. He survived on his own for years before being recruited into the Commission, an agency that kept tabs on the timeline and figures who would threaten it. He eventually betrayed them in order to get back to his time and is constantly hunted by his former employer.[4]
  • Mary J. Blige as Cha-Cha, a ruthless assassin on a mission.[6]
  • Cameron Britton as Hazel, Cha-Cha's partner, who slowly becomes disillusioned with his life as a Commission agent.[7]
  • John Magaro as Leonard Peabody/Harold Jenkins, Vanya's love interest. As a kid, he was an admirer of the Umbrella Academy and begged to join since he was born on the same day but to a normal pregnancy but was humiliated by Reginald. He would later discover Reginald's diary, detailing Vanya's potentials and inserts himself into her life with the goal of manipulating her into discovering and using her powers.[8]
  • Adam Godley as Pogo, a chimpanzee and Reginald's closest assistant.[7]
  • Colm Feore as Sir Reginald Hargreeves / The Monocle, the Umbrella Academy's adoptive alien father and a billionaire industrialist.[7]

Cast-Recurring

  • Sheila McCarthy as Agnes, the owner/baker/waitress at Griddy’s Doughnuts. Hazel’s nascent love interest.
  • Justin H. Min as Ben Hargreeves / Number Six / The Horror, a boy who possesses monsters from other dimensions under his skin. Ben is deceased but appears regularly to Klaus and helps him occasionally.
  • Jordan Claire Robbins as Grace Hargreeves / Mom, the Umbrella Academy's adoptive mother and a robot built by Reginald. She was built after Vanya kept killing nannies with her powers.[9]
  • Ashley Madekwe as Detective Eudora Patch, Diego's former romantic partner.
  • Peter Outerbridge as The Conductor
  • Rainbow Sun Francks as Detective Chuck Beaman
  • Kate Walsh as The Handler, the head of the Commission and Five's former boss.[10]
